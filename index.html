Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about MediScan OCR platform

MediScan supports a wide range of formats including JPG, PNG, PDF, TIFF, and BMP. We can process both single-page and multi-page documents, making it versatile for various medical document types.
Our OCR technology achieves up to 99.9% accuracy on medical documents. We use specialized AI models trained specifically on medical terminology, prescriptions, and healthcare documents to ensure the highest possible accuracy rates.
Yes! MediScan is fully responsive and works on all devices. We also offer native mobile apps for both iOS and Android, allowing you to scan documents directly using your phone's camera.
Security is our top priority. MediScan is fully HIPAA compliant with enterprise-grade encryption. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and we follow strict security protocols to protect sensitive medical information.
Your documents are automatically deleted from our servers after processing unless you choose to save them in your secure account. You have full control over data retention and can delete your data at any time.
